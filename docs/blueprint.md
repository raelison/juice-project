## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FFA500), evoking freshness and energy from citrus fruits.
- Background color: Light beige (#F5F5DC), offering a natural, understated backdrop that lets the product images stand out.
- Accent color: Sunny yellow (#FFD700), highlighting key interactive elements with a friendly pop.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern yet welcoming appeal that works well in both headlines and body text.
- Use clean, line-based icons to represent fruit types and nutritional benefits.
- Employ a grid-based layout for a structured, visually appealing product presentation.
- Use subtle animations for product images and transitions, providing a dynamic browsing experience.
